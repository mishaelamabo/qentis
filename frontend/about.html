Our Mission

Authenticity verification for everyone

In Cameroon across Africa and the World, fake diplomas go undetected, counterfeit medicines harm patients, and fraudulent documents destroy lives. Qentis exists to make verification instant, tamper-proof, and accessible to anyone with no account required, no expensive equipment, and no dependence on any central authority.
